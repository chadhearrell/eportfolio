About Me


Hi, my name is Chad Hearrell. As a member of the Comanche Nation tribe, my Native American heritage adds a unique perspective to my work. I pride myself on being a collaborative and detail-oriented professional, thriving in team-centric environments. Over the course of my extensive career, I have consistently delivered high-fidelity products across diverse Business Units.

Some of my interests include learning about character modeling/animation, Virtual Reality (VR) development, and game development. My hobbies include photography, hiking, and building small games in Unity.

Whether working independently or as part of a team, I bring a multifaceted skill set and a dedication to continuous learning. If you're interested in discovering more about the services that I offer, please feel free to reach out.
